Head Score - Fun But Challenging

DESCRIPTION

A boy bounces soccer ball with his head and try to score Goal. He has to control and balance soccer ball and smash Goal. He can jump and move around to avoid obstacles and collect coins/collectibles to get more time to score Goal.

INSTALL NOW

FEATURES

* Double Jump
* 1 Zone
* 10 Levels
* 10 Training Levels
* Real Ball Physics
* Timer
* Collectibles

RULES

Don't let the ball fall to ground
Balance and Control Ball
Collect Hearts to get more time
Move and Jump player across the platform
Score Goal before run out of time

CONTROLS

Virtual Buttons over the screen

This game is made with Unity 5 Personal Edition. Please write me about any problem or suggestion.
