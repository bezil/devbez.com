Zigit.IO

Cloud Control · Dashboard UX · Product Build

Making server and website management feel simpler.

Zigit.IO is a cloud-control product concept for managing servers, hosting, websites, and WordPress workflows from a cleaner dashboard experience.

SaaS UX Dashboard Design Cloud Control Founder Product

My role

Product direction, UI/UX for cloud-control workflows, frontend presentation, and founder-led product communication.

Product problem

Hosting operations can feel fragmented across panels, servers, updates, and security tasks. Zigit focuses that complexity into a direct management experience.

What I built

  • Dashboard positioning for cloud, server, and WordPress control.
  • Marketing flow that explains hosting management benefits quickly.
  • Information architecture for security, updates, and site operations.

UX decisions

The experience is framed around clarity, fewer steps, visible status, and reducing cognitive load for people managing business websites.
