Motor Glider - Easy and Engaging

DESCRIPTION

A boy with Motor Glider want to land safe before fuel runs out. Help him to land without hitting obstacles. He has to move his Motor Glider in correct direction to land in the winning floor.

INSTALL NOW

FEATURES

* Simple Controls
* 10 Levels
* Timer

RULES

Don't touch obstacles like mountain
Move Glider to appropriate direction
Collect Fuel to increase time
Land Safely to Winning Ground

CONTROLS

*Accelerometer
*Touch Control

This game is made with Unity 5 Personal Edition. Please write me about any problem or suggestion.
