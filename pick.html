Pick - App for Designers

DESCRIPTION

Pick app shows you the latest designs in 2D and 3D. It shows the most popular and recent designs from Dribbble and Sketchfab using their api.

Pick app help designers to get updated with design trends and practices. It also list the top designers based on their designs.
