My role

Product direction, bot UX, Discord command flow design, automation workflow thinking, and launch positioning.

Product problem

Fantasy F1 players and racing fans need fast, contextual race guidance without digging through scattered stats and prediction content.

What I built

  • Command structure for predictions, fantasy picks, and subscription-aware product tiers.

Command UX

  • /predict race: race winner and top-three style predictions.
  • /fantasy: AI-assisted fantasy team picks.
  • /subscribe: tier and upgrade flow for advanced insights.
